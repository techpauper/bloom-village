We are thrilled to announce our annual Bloom Village Summer BBQ! It will be held on July 15th starting at 12:00 PM in the Bloom Village Central Park.

## Event Details:
- **Date**: July 15th, 2024
- **Time**: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- **Location**: Central Park Pavilion
- **Cost**: FREE for all residents

## What to Expect:
There will be catering from local BBQ favorites, bouncy castles for the kids, lawn games, and a neighborhood bake-off competition. This is a wonderful opportunity to connect with neighbors and celebrate our community.

## RSVP:
Please RSVP by July 1st at the HOA office or online.

We look forward to seeing you there!
