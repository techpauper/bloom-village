Following our recent audit of water usage, we have decided to implement new xeriscaping projects along Lavender Lane. These beautiful, sustainable gardens will reduce our common area maintenance costs while increasing curb appeal.

## Project Goals:
- Reduce water consumption by 40%
- Increase biodiversity with native plants
- Create year-round visual interest
- Lower maintenance requirements

## Timeline:
- Design phase: June-July
- Planting phase: August-September
- Establishment phase: Fall 2024

The new gardens will feature drought-resistant perennials, ornamental grasses, and native shrubs that thrive in our climate.
