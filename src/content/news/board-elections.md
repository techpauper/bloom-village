The annual Bloom Village HOA Board elections will be held this fall. We are seeking engaged residents interested in serving on the board.

## Open Positions:
- President
- Vice President
- Treasurer
- Secretary
- 3 Board Member positions

## Timeline:
- Candidate filing deadline: August 1st
- Candidate forum: August 20th
- Mail-in ballot mailing: September 1st
- Election day: September 15th

## How to Get Involved:
- Run for a position: Submit your application by August 1st
- Vote: You must be a property owner in Bloom Village
- Attend the candidate forum: Meet the candidates on August 20th

The HOA Board is responsible for making decisions that affect our entire community. Your participation is valued and appreciated!
