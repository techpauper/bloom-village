Bloom Village will undergo a comprehensive road resurfacing program this summer. Rose Gardens and Tulip Hill will undergo road resurfacing during the week of June 10th-24th.

## What You Need to Know:
- Please ensure all vehicles are parked in driveways - not on the street during these dates to avoid towing
- Work hours will be 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Residents will be notified 48 hours in advance for each street
- Emergency access will be maintained at all times

## Project Benefits:
This maintenance will improve road safety, reduce potholes, and enhance the overall appearance of our community. The new surface will last approximately 15-20 years with proper maintenance.
