We're excited to announce that the Tulip Hill community pool will officially open on June 25th for the summer season!

## Pool Hours:
- Weekdays: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Weekends: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

## Important Rules:
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
- No glass containers allowed
- Please shower before entering

## Safety Features:
- Certified lifeguards on duty during all hours
- First aid station on site
- Shallow and deep end for all ages
- ADA accessible entrance

Come enjoy a refreshing summer at your neighborhood pool!
