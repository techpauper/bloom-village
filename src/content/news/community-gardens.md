Bloom Village is proud to announce the launch of our new Community Garden Initiative! Sunflower Square residents can now sign up for dedicated garden plots.

## Garden Details:
- 10x10 foot plots available
- Located in Sunflower Square Community Area
- Tools and water provided
- Organic gardening required

## Sign-Up Process:
1. Complete application at the HOA office
2. Pay seasonal fee ($25/season)
3. Attend mandatory orientation
4. Begin planting!

## Schedule:
- Sign-ups open: June 1st - June 30th
- Orientations: July 1st-7th
- Planting season: July 8th - September 30th

This is a wonderful opportunity to grow your own vegetables, herbs, and flowers while connecting with fellow residents!
